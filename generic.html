Who am I?

I'm passionate about data and insights, looking to make it actionable
for driving growth and decision-making.

I'm curious by nature and motivated by impact. I'm currently looking to grow in roles that blend technical BI development with commercial and strategic understanding — helping teams make better, faster, and more confident data-driven decisions.
